Speaking

I’ve been sharing my love for my profession and the Web with talks on UI engineering at events and conferences all over the globe since 2014. I’ve given talks, keynotes, and run workshops at events such as An Event Apart, Web Directions, CSSConf, dotCSS, CSSDay, Fronteers, Microsoft’s Edge Summit, Mozilla’s View Source, and many others across the world.

Below is a list of all my past and upcoming speaking engagements. If you’d like to invite me to speak at your event, please read my speaker rider first and the drop me a line at hello@sarasoueidan.com.
